NourishNook Cafe

Our Mission

To NourishNook Cafe provides students, faculty, and staff with calthy, affordable, and delicious meals. We are committed to sustainability, and we source our ingredients from local farmers and vendors whenever possible.

Our History

The NourishNook Cafe opened in 2009. It was built to serve the growing student population at the Sugar Land campus. Over the years, it has become a beloved gathering place for students, faculty, and staff, and it is known for its friendly service and diverse menu.
